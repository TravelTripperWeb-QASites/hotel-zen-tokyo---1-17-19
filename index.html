About Hotel Zen Tokyo

{% endregionblock %} {% regionblock about_hotel_title, type:header %}

Stay Minimal, Experience Maximal

{% endregionblock %} {% regionblock about_hotel_detail, type:html %}

Hotel Zen Tokyo's guest experience is crafted with the spirit of Japanese zen minimalism in mind. We have designed the most modern, compact rendition of traditional Japanese architecture in hopes to provide our guests with an opportunity to experience Japan to the fullest, with comfort, and at reasonable prices. Travellers enjoy a fusion of efficient capsule accommodations with authentic traditional design - in essence, a modern and urban ryokan experience in the heart of Tokyo.

Minimalistic Room Design

{% endregionblock %} {% regionblock room_title, type:header %}

Teahouse-inspired Zen Pods

{% endregionblock %}
{% regionblock room_detail_link, type:html %} {% t 'discover_more' %} {% endregionblock %}
{% regionblock room_detail, type:html %}

Each room in the hotel is a pod (capsule-type accommodation) and guests can choose from various bed sizes and pod styles. Each pod is designed in accordance with Japanese traditional design disciplines, with touches of wabi-sabi, Japanese version of minimalistic design that appreciates small spaces and natural materials. Enjoy a good night’s stay in traditional Japanese architecture while enjoying music at the bar downstairs or local flavors while strolling around the neighborhood.

Our hotel is mere steps away from Ningyocho Station, which offers direct access to both of Tokyo's airports and the city's most exciting destinations. The Ningyocho/Nihonmbashi neighborhood is a historically rich district within Tokyo that has retained much of its traditional charm, architecture and experiences. Take a stroll around the hotel and enjoy some of Tokyo's oldest shops and local foods.
